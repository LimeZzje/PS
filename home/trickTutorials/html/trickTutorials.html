Gotta Learn 'Em All!

  • This list is entierly subjective but it should give you an idea of what tricks to learn in which order.

  • Only once you learned a trick in all slots, reverse, palm down/palm normal (if possible), you have mastered it.
    Learning tricks in reverse and in all slots is as important as learning the trick normal in only one slot!!! Make sure you dont miss out on that.

  • For example: You just learned "Sonic 23-12": now try to learn "Sonic reverse 12-23", "Sonic 34-23", "Sonic Reverse 23-34" (recommended order). This works for almost all tricks.

  • "Sonic 23-12"?! - The numbers mason, what do they mean?! (Guideline on notation for tricks)
