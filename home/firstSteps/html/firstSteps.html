Before we talk about the basics let’s get some FAQ out of the way:

„Do I need one of those weird, big pens to get started“?

No, you can learn your frist tricks with regular pens. No Problem. As soon as you are done learning the fundamentals with a regular pen and you dont HATE learning these tricks, you might want to consider buying (or modding) a proper pen. It´ll make your learning a lot easier

„Where do I get those pens?“

I always encourage beginners to make their own Pens. It´s usually the cheapest way and you get your first modding experience. It´s easy. Just go to your local stationary store, look for a round, doublecapped marker, get some grips and tips from ballpoint pens, put the grips onto the caps of your marker, put the tips inside the grips and DONE!

Premade pens from Amazon are usually pretty bad spinning and quality wise, sadly. Buying from Amazon should be your last option.

There are some professional online penspinning-shops but they tend do be a bit pricy.

Check out the „Penmodding“-section if you want to know more about penmods.

„Is penspinning hard to learn?“

It’s not an easy hobby by any means. You can’t just pick up Penspinning and master it right away.

You need to learn all tricks one by one and learn to combine them.

On the bright side, learning new tricks is very fun and it is nice to watch your trick repertoire grow weekly.

Also, don’t get discouraged by slow progress in the beginning. Patience is key.

„I don’t think my fingers are flexible enough to do that… “

Everybody can learn penspinning. You don‘t need special fingers to be an elite spinner.

But keep in mind: It might stress your fingers and tendons If you have a very heavy pen, especially in the beginning. It can lead to tendonitis (Google it) if you overdo yourself, so take some breaks inbetween long sessions of spinning.

…Still here after reading the FAQs? Great! Let’s dive in!

Penspinning is a sequence of single tricks, you have to learn one by one in order to combine them into cool „combos“. Terminology is a big topic so you can understand and talk about the actions seen in videos.
